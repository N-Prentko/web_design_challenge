Cloudiness

The purpose of this visual is to faciliate the inquirer interested in the relationship between a city's latitudinal coordinate, and the percentage of cloudiness found in it's sky. Specifically, this visual will give him insight into cloudiness pertaining to 1/5/2017. The percentage of cloudiness per city in relationship to it's latitudinal coordinate almost seems irrelevant because of the wide range of percentages of cloudiness for cities at or near the same latitudinal coordinate. However, it is noteworthy that there is a heavy concentration of cities that had skys with a 0% cloudiness at a plurality of the coordinates in question. The main takeaway for the inquirer is that, no matter where he is on the globe, he can not predict with certainty the amount of cloud coverage there will be on his latitudal coordinate alone.
